1. Check your sources for inaccuracies, misrepresentation, and skewed reporting
2. When reporting, make sure to properly represent your data set (in size and demographic)
3. Dont oversimplify the conclusion. Say what the data means, the problems it points to, the larger system at play, and follow up questions that need their own reporting
